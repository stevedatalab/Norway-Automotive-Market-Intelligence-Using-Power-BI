"In year 2015 towards 2017, Norway did something no other nation has: As an analyst, I didn't just want to see the headline; I wanted to see the mechanics of this shift. This repository contains the end-to-end data pipeline—from raw registration logs to a high-fidelity business intelligence suite—designed to answer 7 critical business requirements."

a) Display the units sold for each car brand? 

b) How much revenue was generated for each car model?

c) Can you display no of variants available for each car model? 

d) What is the market value for each car brand? Show with the help of a BAR chart.

e) Display the top 5 models(variants) for each brand based on revenue generated?

f) Show the market share percentage of brands.

g) Show the trend of sales over time for each car model.

The finding were:
1. The total units sold were [1347K]
2. Total revenue generated was [$1.2bn]
3. The total variant for each car model was [146]
4. Market share % was  [28.76%- Volkswagen, 23.4%-Toyota, 13.88%-Ford, 10.20%-BMW, 9.81%-Audi, 9.19%-Skoda, 4.77%-Hyundai]
5. The brand market value for Volkswagen was high followed by BMW and Hyundai the with lowest value.
6. The top 5 models based on revenue were: Golf, Fiesta,Focus,3 series,and Tiguan.
7. The trend sales increases from 2007-2016 then decreases from 2016-2017.
